Meal

  • Our grand Thanksgiving feast includes traditional roast turkey with all the trimmings plus turkey gravy, our famous carved prime rib with aujou, roast salmon with dijon dill cream sauce, veal stuffed ravioli in a creamy wild mushroom sauce, and butternut squash ravioli with butter sage.
