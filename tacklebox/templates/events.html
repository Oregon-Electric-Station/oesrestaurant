Private Events

At OES we combine a fantastic location, organic locally sourced food passionately crafted, with a team that is dedicated to the fine art of hospitality in a classic and modern atmosphere to provide an unforgettable event.

OES offers the possibility from 10 to 300 people maximum for seated parties, and the possibility from 10 to 600 people maximum for cocktails parties. OES is the perfect place for exhibitions, presentations, weddings, business meetings or just friendly gatherings. We have different rooms ready to accommodate your functions needs.

We offer different styles of service, from sit down set menus, to buffet or family style, and cocktail receptions.

We welcome accommodating you in any way you need with special requests from themed decorations to different styles of cuisine, it would be our pleasure to assist you. Please feel free to contact us with any questions or requests.

Thank you for your interest and we look forward to hearing from you.
